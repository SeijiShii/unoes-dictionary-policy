## How to Cancel Your Subscription

UNOES Dictionary subscriptions can be **fully canceled from within the app**.

### Cancellation Steps (From the App)

1. **Open the "Subscription Status" screen in the app**
   - Select "Subscription Status" from the app menu

2. **Tap the "Stop Auto-Renewal" button**
   - A confirmation dialog will appear

3. **Confirm and execute cancellation**
   - Tap "Confirm" in the confirmation dialog to stop auto-renewal
   - Cancellation is immediately reflected in Google Play Store

### Cancellation Steps (From Google Play Store)

If you cannot cancel from within the app or prefer to cancel directly from Google Play Store, you can cancel using the following steps:

1. **Access Google Play Store's purchase management page**
   - You can access Google Play Store's purchase management page from the following link:
   - [Open Google Play Purchase Management Page](https://play.google.com/store/account/subscriptions)
   - Or open the Google Play Store app and go to "Account" â†’ "Purchases and subscriptions"

2. **Select your subscription**
   - Select the "UNOES Dictionary" subscription on the purchase management page

3. **Stop auto-renewal**
   - Select "Cancel subscription" or "Stop auto-renewal"

### Important Notes

- **You can continue using the service until expiration after cancellation**
  - Even after stopping subscription auto-renewal, you can continue using the service until the current expiration date
  - After the expiration date, service access will be restricted

- **About Refunds**
  - Refunds for subscriptions follow Google Play Store's refund policy
  - For details, please check the [Google Play Refund Policy](https://support.google.com/googleplay/answer/2479637)

- **Cancellation from within the app**
  - You can fully cancel from the "Subscription Status" screen within the app
  - Cancellation is immediately reflected in Google Play Store, and auto-renewal is stopped
  - You can also cancel from Google Play Store's purchase management page

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Will I lose access to the service immediately after canceling?

A: No. You can continue using the service until the current expiration date after canceling. After the expiration date, service access will be restricted.

### Q: Can I subscribe again after canceling?

A: Yes. You can subscribe again at any time. You can apply from the "Subscription" screen within the app.

### Q: Where can I cancel my subscription?

A: You can fully cancel from the "Subscription Status" screen within the app. You can also cancel from Google Play Store's purchase management page. Both methods reflect cancellation immediately.
